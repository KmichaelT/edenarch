Hello, I am Eden

Architect from Addis Ababa

Hello, I'm Eden Eskinder, a skilled architect and urban planner with a passion for creating functional and aesthetically pleasing spaces. With years of experience in the field, I am proficient in designing floor plan drawings and 3d models, as well as producing photorealistic visualizations for presentations and marketing purposes. I have a keen eye for color and texture, and I excel at selecting wall and textile colors and arranging layouts to optimize space and enhance the user experience. As a team player, I value collaboration and strive for the highest quality of work, while maintaining open communication with clients and colleagues. My expertise extends to furniture selection and placement, ensuring that each design is optimized for comfort, functionality, and style. With a strong focus on sustainability, I am committed to creating environmentally friendly and socially responsible designs. Let's work together to bring your vision to life.

My Services

Design and Planning

This service involves designing and planning buildings and other physical structures, such as parks, urban landscapes, and public spaces.

Interior Design

This service involves designing the interiors of buildings, including the selection of materials, furnishings, and finishes.

Landscape Design

This service involves designing the outdoor areas surrounding a building or structure, including gardens, parks, and other green spaces.

Building Information Modeling

Creating detailed 3D models of buildings and structures for more efficient collaboration between architects, engineers, and construction teams.

Renovation and Adaptive Reuse

This involves renovating and repurposing existing buildings and structures, including historic buildings, to meet modern design standards and functionality.

Consulting Services

This service involves advising clients on best design practices, including energy-efficient systems, and environmentally friendly materials.
