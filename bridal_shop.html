Bridal Showroom

  • Ms Nardos
  • January, 2023

This interior design project was completed for Nardos, a bridal showroom/shop. The entire space, including the furniture and display area, was designed by me using Revit, Can, and Lumen. To create a functional and elegant space, I utilized various skills such as spatial planning, color coordination, and lighting design. My objective was to balance the practical needs of the business with the aesthetics of the bridal industry.

The project spanned two and a half weeks and involved meticulous planning and execution of every detail. The end result was a beautiful showroom that captures the essence of the bridal experience. Soft lighting, neutral tones, and strategic use of space create a comfortable and welcoming environment for clients. The custom-designed furniture adds a touch of luxury and sophistication to the space. Throughout the project, I ensured that every element was tailored to meet the specific needs of the client and exceeded their expectations. This project highlights my expertise in interior design, including spatial planning, color coordination, and lighting design.
