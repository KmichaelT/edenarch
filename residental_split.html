Residental (split-level)

  • Mr Wonde
  • November, 2022

This is one of my latest architectural design project - a residential building for Mr. Wonde. As the sole designer, I had the opportunity to use my skills in Revit, CAD, and Lumen to create a stunning and functional home for my client.

The project took three weeks from start to finish, and involved every aspect of design from initial sketches to final 3D renderings. The result is a beautiful, modern space that perfectly suits Mr. Wonde's needs. Through my expertise in these programs, I was able to create a space that is not only visually stunning, but also practical and comfortable. I'm excited to share this portfolio piece as a representation of my ability to bring clients' visions to life through my architectural design skills.
